RajeevFest

A Celebration of Rajeev Alur

DATE: TBD (tentatively late July, co-located with FLoC 2026)

This workshop celebrates the contributions of Rajeev Alur (on the occasion of his 60th birthday) to the theory and applications of logic in computer science, computer-aided verification and the foundations of computer science. In addition to celebrating his momentous achievements, this workshop will also honor Rajeev’s personal engagement with and service to the FLOC and broader CS communities. We hope to see many of you there!
